Frequently Asked Questions

You have questions, we have answers.

Lifetime

How does the Lifetime plan work?

Our Lifetime plan grants you lifetime access to all of our plugin updates and premium support, instead of having to renew your license key.

Yearly

How does the Yearly plan work?

If you choose our yearly plan, you will need to renew your license key in order to receive further updates and premium support.

Yearly

Will my website break if I don’t renew my license key?

No, your website will remain intact. Once you purchase GP Premium, you own it for life and are free to use it for as long as you like. The only difference is support and updates.

General

What kind of support can I expect?

We’re proud to offer industry-leading support in our premium support forums. We’re always happy to help!

General

How many sites can I use GP Premium on?

Your license can be activated on up to 500 websites, whether they’re for you or your clients.

General

What do updates include?

Updates include new modules, improved features, performance enhancements, and bug fixes.

General

What’s your refund policy?

If you’re not happy with GP Premium, we’ll happily provide you with a refund within 30 days of your purchase – no questions asked!

Yearly

Will my Yearly Plan license key renew automatically?

No. You’ll never be billed after your initial purchase. After 1 year, you can choose to manually renew your license key.

General

Can I upgrade from the Yearly plan to the Lifetime plan?

Yes! You can upgrade from the yearly plan to the lifetime plan inside your account at a prorated cost.
